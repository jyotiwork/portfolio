The Problem

The challenge was to identify a problem in the area of “Education and Learning” caused by the COVID - 19 pandemic and design an impactful, innovative and feasible solution that uses digital technology to solve the identified problem. I chose to understand and empathise with students pursuing higher education in India and uncover the problems faced by them. According to UNESCO, about 32 crore learners were affected in India. Out of which about 3.4 crore learners were pursuing higher education.


The Solution

Spot is an app for college students to track, learn, socialise and network with friends, faculties and industry mentors. It also offers books and courses. Basically, everything you need to get through college at one spot.​​​​​​​


DESIGN PROCESS​​​​​​​


The Double Diamond

The double diamond framework acted as a roadmap to design for this problem. It helped me to first diverge and dig deep into the problem area by conducting  both primary and secondary research then to converge from observations to insights to a HMW statement. Further diverging and brainstorming ideas and finally converging to a solution.






DISCOVER​​​​​​​


Secondary Research

According to UNESCO, about 32 crore learners were affected in India. Out of which about 3.4 crore learners were pursuing higher education.





Primary Research

I conducted a quantitative survey which was filled by 70+ college going students of UG and PG to understand what they liked and disliked about college both in offline and online scenario. I conducted about 5 in-depth interviews to understand their pain points and empathise further.



Observations

▶ 66% students would like a proper feedback system for their assignments.
▶ 73% students feel industry mentoring should be incorporated more in their current curriculum.
▶ 47% students desire 1:1 mentoring.
▶ 48% of them would like an online library archive.
▶ 62% students would like online skill based courses to be a part of college.
▶ 66% students like peer reviews.
▶ Students want to be able to access to their scores, study material, attendance, everything college related, all the time.


DEFINE​​​​​​​


Persona Mapping

Persona mapping helped me understand the needs, wants and frustrations of our target users. It helped immensely in designing for them and always keeping them at the heart of every step in the design process.

Target Audience: Under graduate and Post graduate students in India.




DEFINE​​​​​​​


Key Insights

▶ Students need constant feedback on their work from teachers and peers in order to understand and learn from their mistakes.
▶ Students believe having a constant connect with someone from the industry in the form of mentoring or networking would keep unto date with industry requirements and give them regular reality checks.
▶ Students find self paced learning convenient hence like their study material to be always accessible to them.



HMW Statement

How might we make higher education for students more effective and efficient in the new normal?​​​​​​​


DEVELOP​​​​​​​


Brainstorming

The HMW statement gave room to a lot of ideas, including some speculative ones




Impact vs Feasibility Matrix

In order to decide the best possible solution among the rest, I made an impact vs feasibility prioritisation matrix to decide on the most feasible and impactful solution.

Conclusion

COVID - 19 Pandemic took an unprecedented test on education. It was tough but technology and products are here to share the load, so is Spot. This product is valid and needed irrespective of a pandemic or not, it will make students’ lives easier, college is hard enough already.



Testing

Tested with 8 people out of which 75% (6 people) were satisfied.
Suggestions from users:
▶ If the user is adding a product in the cart, it should show whether it is in the kitchen or not/ last added.
▶ If the product user is adding in the cart is unhealthy, give warning.
▶ Features like food journal with personal comments, community of people pursuing healthy lifestyle could be added.
▶ Micro-animation and illustration could be added.
There is a lot of scope for reiteration.



Key Learnings

I learned a lot more about people's behaviour and their reasons through this project. Also, learnt how important it is to reiterate, keep users at the heart of everything we do and use the double diamond framework better.
