KnowMart

SIDE PROJECT • USER RESEARCH • PRODUCT DESIGN


The COVID-19 lockdown produced behavioral, psychosocial and environmental changes which has led to widespread rapid weight gain amongst people due to an increase in food shopping, consumption and sedentary lifestyle.
This was a classroom assignment which I reiterated into a side project. I conducted quantitative surveys, interviewed people (age group:19-23 ) to learn about change in their behavior, consumption and lifestyle due to the lockdown and designed KnowMart; a product with online grocery shopping services with integrated meal planning which helps people become more aware of what they are buying and eating.

The Problem

There has been a rapid weight gain amongst people in the COVID-19 lockdown due to an increase in food shopping, consumption and sedentary lifestyle which stem from the behavioral, psychosocial and environmental changes caused by the lockdown.


The Solution

KnowMart is an app which provides online grocery shopping services with integrated meal planning to help people become mindful of what they are buying and eating. It also helps people have better dietary quality, cook diversified meals, save money and also addresses the issue of not knowing what to prepare for meals. ​​​​​​​


DESIGN PROCESS​​​​​​​


The Double Diamond

The double diamond framework acted as a roadmap to design for this problem. It helped me to first diverge and dig deep into the problem area by conducting both primary and secondary research then to converge from observations to insights to a HMW statement. Further diverging and brainstorming ideas and finally converging to a solution.






DISCOVER​​​​​​​


Secondary Research

I read journals, articles and papers about the changes in human behavior, lifestyle, consumer trends during the lockdown which further led me to reading about weight gain/loss, nutrition and mindful eating. Some of the key findings for the project:

▶ People have gained weight due to an increase in food shopping, consumption and sedentary lifestyle in the lockdown.
▶ While people are trying to eat healthy to strengthen their immune system against the virus, due to stress and anxiety, they are binge eating as well.
▶ Healthy, balanced and mindful eating should not be a fad diet but inculcated as a part of people's lifestyle.



Observations

▶ 44% people shop for groceries online only.
▶ 37% people gained weight during the lockdown.
▶ 50% people used to exercise at the beginning of the lockdown but have stopped now.
▶ 37% people binge more now whereas 42% people have started eating more mindfully now.
▶ 50% of the people have tried meal planning.
▶ People are more likely to check for ingredients/nutrients in the product than calories.

The Solution

KnowMart is an app which provides online grocery shopping services with integrated meal planning. It helps users in the following ways:

Primary:
▶ Becoming mindful of what they are buying and eating
▶ Having better dietary quality because nutrition information about everything is just a tap away.

Secondary:
▶ Saving money since they know what they need to buy.
​​​​​​​▶ Not having to think "What to cook next?"


DELIVER​​​​​​​


Login and Onboarding

▶ ​​​​​​​Keeping the user informed what our app is about and what all to expect.
Skip option is important because:
▶ Users might just want to explore your app first.
▶ They might not trust you with their information, not just yet.

Login and Onboarding


Personalisation

▶ Users can add items already in their kitchen, they may also connect it with smart appliances to reduce their work.
▶ Food gets divided into categories for user's convenience.
▶ ​​​​​​​Before making a meal plan, user needs to answer few questions to make the experience personalized and avoid error.

Personalisation


Meal Planning Feature

▶ Meal plans are divided in tabs based on the time of the day and cards for different meals which lead to recipes.
▶ Cards provide important information about nutrition, time and serves.
▶ ​​​​​​​Option of alternative meal to give control to the user over their meal plan, they can also add/save their own recipes.

Meal Planning Feature


Testing

Tested with 8 people out of which 75% (6 people) were satisfied.
Suggestions from users:
▶ If the user is adding a product in the cart, it should show whether it is in the kitchen or not/ last added.
▶ If the product user is adding in the cart is unhealthy, give warning.
▶ Features like food journal with personal comments, community of people pursuing healthy lifestyle could be added.
▶ Micro-animation and illustration could be added.
There is a lot of scope for reiteration.



Key Learnings

I learned a lot more about people's behaviour and their reasons through this project. Also, learnt how important it is to reiterate, keep users at the heart of everything we do and use the double diamond framework better.
